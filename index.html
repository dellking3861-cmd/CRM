Discuss two ways seeds are dispersed

Model Teacher Answer

Seeds are dispersed through mechanisms such as: (1) Wind (light seeds float on air currents), (2) Water (seeds float to new banks), (3) Animals (clinging to fur or being eaten and excreted), and (4) Explosions (pods burst open throwing seeds).

Challenge: 2 Marks
33

Explain how fewer insects affect plant reproduction

Model Teacher Answer

Fewer insects mean there are less active pollinators to transfer pollen from anthers to stigmas. This decreases fertilization rates and results in fewer seeds being produced.
